## üõ°Ô∏è Solutions & Best Practices

### 1. Expertise-Based Decision Rights

**Principle:**
```
Decision Authority = Relevant Expertise
NOT just equity ownership
```

**Implementation:**
```
Technical decisions ‚Üí CTO decides
Marketing decisions ‚Üí CMO decides
Strategy decisions ‚Üí Joint decision
Financial decisions ‚Üí CFO input critical
```

---

### 2. Formal Decision Processes

**Create Clear Rules:**
```
Decision Type ‚Üí Decision Maker
‚Ä¢ Routine ops ‚Üí Respective leader
‚Ä¢ Strategy ‚Üí Full team vote
‚Ä¢ Hiring ‚Üí Relevant manager + approval
‚Ä¢ Major pivots ‚Üí Unanimous or 2/3
```

---

### 3. Psychological Safety

**Build Culture Where:**
‚úì Everyone can speak up
‚úì Ideas judged on merit
‚úì Disagreement is safe
‚úì Expertise is respected

**Practices:**
- Round-robin speaking
- Anonymous feedback
- Devil's advocate role
- "Yes, and..." not "No, but..."

---

### 4. Regular Power Audits

**Questions to Ask:**
```
1. Who actually makes decisions?
2. Whose ideas get implemented?
3. Who gets interrupted vs. heard?
4. Is power aligned with expertise?
5. Do all members feel valued?
```

---

### 5. Equity + Authority Alignment

**Better Structure:**

‚Ä¢ Authority = Decision-making
