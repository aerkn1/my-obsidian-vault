## ğŸ“Š Franchise Structure

### Financial Model

**Initial Costs (Franchisee Pays):**
- Franchise fee: $20K - $50K (one-time)
- Setup costs: $100K - $500K+
- Equipment, build-out, inventory

**Ongoing Costs (Franchisee Pays):**
- Royalty fee: 4-8% of gross revenue
- Marketing fee: 2-4% of gross revenue
- Supply purchases (often from franchisor)

**Revenue (Franchisor):**
- Franchise fees
- Ongoing royalties
- Supply sales
- Marketing fund
