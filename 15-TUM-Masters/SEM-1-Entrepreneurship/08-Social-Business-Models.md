## üéØ Three Types of Social Business Models

Social enterprises create impact through different mechanisms:

1. **Social by Sourcing** - HOW they make it
2. **Social by Selling** - WHAT they sell / TO WHOM
3. **Social by Profit Sharing** - WHERE profits go

---

## üéì Exam Question & Answer

### Question
**Which of the following best describes a "Social by sourcing" business model?**

a) Organizations that make their impact through what they sell or to whom they sell it
b) Organizations that share some or all of their profits with charitable organizations or causes
c) Organizations that develop their programs by how they make their products or services
d) Organizations that focus only on maximizing financial profit

**‚úÖ ANSWER: (c)**

**Explanation**:
- **Social by Sourcing** = **HOW** they make products/services
- Focus on ethical production, supply chain, employment
- **(a)** = Social by Selling (WHAT/TO WHOM)
- **(b)** = Social by Profit Sharing (WHERE)
- **(d)** = Not social at all
