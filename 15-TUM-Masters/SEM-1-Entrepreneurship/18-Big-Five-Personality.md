## üéØ The Big Five Model

The **Big Five** (also called **OCEAN** or **Five-Factor Model**) is the most scientifically validated personality framework.

```
O - Openness to Experience
C - Conscientiousness
E - Extraversion
A - Agreeableness
N - Neuroticism
```

**NOT included**: Proactivity, Intelligence, Risk-taking, Creativity

---

## üéì Exam Question & Answer

### Question
**Which of these is NOT a big five personality trait?**

a) Openness
b) Neuroticism
c) Conscientiousness
d) Proactivity

**‚úÖ ANSWER: (d)**

**Explanation**:
- Big Five = **OCEAN**: Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism
- **Proactivity** is a behavioral tendency, NOT a Big Five trait
- This is a common trap question!
