## ğŸ­ Common Manipulation Techniques

### 1. Revenue Recognition Fraud

**Technique**: Record revenue before earned

**Examples**:
- âŒ Recording sales before goods shipped
- âŒ "Channel stuffing" - forcing distributors to buy
- âŒ Recording fake sales
- âŒ Early recognition of long-term contracts

**Red Flags**:
- Revenue growing faster than industry
- Accounts receivable growing faster than sales
- Large sales at quarter-end

### 2. Expense Manipulation

**Technique**: Delay or hide expenses

**Examples**:
- âŒ Capitalizing expenses as assets (should be expensed immediately)
- âŒ Failing to record impairments (your discovery!)
- âŒ Underestimating warranty costs
- âŒ Hiding repairs as "improvements"

### 3. Asset Overvaluation

**Technique**: Keep assets at inflated values

**Examples**:
- âŒ No depreciation (should be systematic)
- âŒ Avoiding impairment losses
- âŒ Inflating inventory values
- âŒ Fictitious assets (Wirecard!)

### 4. Liability Understatement

**Technique**: Hide or minimize obligations

**Examples**:
- âŒ Off-balance-sheet financing

- âŒ Not recording contingent liabilities
- âŒ Hiding debt in subsidiaries
