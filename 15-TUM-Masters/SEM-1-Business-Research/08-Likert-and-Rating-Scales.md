## ğŸ¨ Likert Scale Characteristics

### Properties

**1. Ordinal Level**
- Ordered categories
- Distances between points not necessarily equal
- But often treated as interval in practice

**2. Closed Response Options**
- Predefined choices
- Must select from given options

**3. Symmetric Scale**
- Equal positive and negative options
- Usually includes neutral midpoint

**4. Typical Length**
- 5-point: Most common
- 7-point: More discrimination
- 3-point: Quick, less precise
