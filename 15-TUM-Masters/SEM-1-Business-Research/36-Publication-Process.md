## ğŸ“‹ The Process

### 1. Author Submits
- Write manuscript
- Follow journal guidelines
- Submit online

### 2. Editor Screening
- Desk reject or send to review
- 30-50% desk rejected

### 3. Peer Review
- 2-3 reviewers
- Anonymous (usually)
- 2-6 months

### 4. Decision
- **Accept** (rare on first submission)
- **Revise & Resubmit** (R&R)
- **Reject**

### 5. Revision
- **Authors** revise (not reviewers!)
- Respond to each comment
- Resubmit

### 6. Further Reviews
- May go through multiple rounds
- Eventually accept or reject
