### Median
**Definition**: Middle value when sorted

**Use**: Ordinal data, skewed distributions

**Example**: Ages: 20, 22, 25, 30, 100

### Mode
**Definition**: Most frequent value

**Use**: Nominal data, any distribution

**Example**: Colors: Red, Red, Blue, Green, Red
Mode = Red

## ðŸ“ˆ Distribution Shape

### Skewness
- **Positive**: Long right tail (mean > median)
- **Negative**: Long left tail (mean < median)
- **Zero**: Symmetric

### Kurtosis
- **Leptokurtic**: Peaked, heavy tails
- **Platykurtic**: Flat, light tails
- **Mesokurtic**: Normal
