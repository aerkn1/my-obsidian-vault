## ðŸ”„ The Process

### 1. Show Efficacy
Does the practice work?
- Rigorous research
- Causal evidence

### 2. Collect Evidence
Systematic literature review
- What does research say?
- Quality of evidence?

### 3. Aggregate Evidence
Meta-analysis, systematic review
- Combine findings
- Overall pattern?

### 4. Develop Guidelines
Best practices for managers
- Based on evidence
- Practical recommendations

## ðŸ’¡ Example

**Question**: Does remote work increase productivity?

**Evidence-Based Approach**:
1. **Efficacy**: Conduct rigorous studies
2. **Collect**: Systematic review of all studies
3. **Aggregate**: Meta-analyze effects
4. **Guidelines**: "Remote work increases productivity 10-15% for knowledge workers when..."

**vs. Opinion-Based**:
- "I think remote work is bad"
- "My experience says..."
