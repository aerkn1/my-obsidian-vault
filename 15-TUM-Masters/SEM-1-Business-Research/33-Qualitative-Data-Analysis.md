### 1st Order Analysis
**Informant-centric**: Stay close to data
- Interview quotes
- Observation descriptions
- Participants' language

### 2nd Order Analysis
**Researcher-centric**: Theoretical lens
- Group 1st order into categories
- More abstract
- Conceptual themes

### Aggregate Dimensions
**Theory development**:
- High-level constructs
- Connect to literature
- Build model
